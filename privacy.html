Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This app does not share or store user data beyond necessary for functionality.

Data We Collect

  • Account information (name, email)
  • Message content received through connected integrations (WhatsApp/Instagram)
  • Technical logs and usage metrics

How We Use Your Data

  • Displaying and responding to messages
  • Error troubleshooting and security
  • Product improvement

Third-Party Sharing

Data is only shared with infrastructure providers necessary for service delivery (e.g., hosting, database, Meta/WhatsApp API). It is not shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Data is retained for reasonable periods in accordance with legal obligations and legitimate interests. Can be deleted upon request.

Your Rights

  • Request access and correction
  • Request deletion
  • Object to processing

Contact

For privacy-related requests, please contact us at support@aide.app.

Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and data, please send an email to support@aide.app with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
